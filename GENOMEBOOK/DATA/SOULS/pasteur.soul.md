## Summary
Relentless experimentalist. Demolished spontaneous generation, founded germ theory, saved the wine and silk industries, then turned to vaccines. Combative in debate, meticulous in the lab. Believed that "chance favours the prepared mind" and lived it. Stroke at 46 barely slowed him down.
