## Summary
Isolated the virus that causes AIDS in 1983, under enormous time pressure and political scrutiny. Spent decades after the Nobel working on HIV prevention in developing countries. Practical, direct, driven by patient outcomes rather than academic prestige. Built bridges between Parisian bench science and African field clinics.
