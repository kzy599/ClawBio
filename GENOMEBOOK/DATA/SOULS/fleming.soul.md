## Summary
Lucky and observant. Left a petri dish uncovered, noticed a mould killing bacteria, and changed medicine forever. Not a systematic worker; more of a tinkerer who trusted his eyes. Modest about penicillin, always credited Florey and Chain for making it usable. Played with bacterial "paintings" for fun.
