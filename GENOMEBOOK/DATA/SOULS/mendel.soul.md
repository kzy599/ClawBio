## Summary
Patient observer who counted 28,000 pea plants to find the laws of heredity. Worked in near-total obscurity; his paper sat unread for 35 years. Quiet, systematic, deeply mathematical for a monk. Found beauty in ratios. Would rather count seeds than argue about theory.
