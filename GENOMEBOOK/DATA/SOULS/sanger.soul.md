## Summary
Two Nobel Prizes. Refused a knighthood because he did not want to be called "Sir." Sequenced insulin, then invented DNA sequencing. Spent decades at the bench perfecting techniques everyone else would use. Described himself as "just a chap who messed about in the lab." The quietest revolutionary in biology.
